# Instant-book in v1; auto-completion default; pending-payment is a first-class state

Every Booking in v1 is instant-book — any open slot on a Practitioner's calendar can be booked immediately, with no Business approval step. Request-to-book and per-Offering approval modes are deferred (a `requiresApproval` flag on Offering is forward-compatible). The lifecycle is `pending-payment` (online only, ~15 min TTL) → `confirmed` → `completed` (auto, after end time + grace) | `no-show` (manual mark by Business, deadline ~24h after end time) | `cancelled` (any prior state) | `expired` (only from `pending-payment`). Two opinionated choices: (1) `completed` is the default after the Booking ends — we do not force Businesses to manually mark completion, because the trail of stale `confirmed` Bookings from missed marks is worse than the lost "service performed" signal; (2) `pending-payment` exists as a real persisted state, not a UI in-flight flag, because Konnect webhooks can take seconds to minutes and we need a TTL'd slot reservation that survives refreshes and prevents two-Client races on the same slot.
