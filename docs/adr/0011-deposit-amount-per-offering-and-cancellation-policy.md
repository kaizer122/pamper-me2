# Online payment is deposit-based with a Business-configurable amount per Offering

When the Client picks online as the PaymentMethod, they pay a Deposit at booking time — a Money amount set by the Business per Offering, between a platform-defined floor and the full price. Setting `deposit = price` is full prepayment; smaller values leave a balance to settle at the Venue. The cancellation policy uses the Deposit as the natural lever: free cancellation up to a Business-configurable cutoff (default 24h before the Booking, refund automatic via Konnect), Deposit forfeited on late cancellation and no-show. Forfeited Deposits are split per the same commission rules as a completed Booking — no special revenue path. We rejected platform-wide fixed-percentage deposits (don't match the cost spread between a 50 TND manicure and a 400 TND balayage) and full-prepay-only (too aggressive for a market that defaults to cash-at-venue). The "forfeit splits commission" rule was chosen over "Business keeps 100%" because it keeps Business and platform incentives aligned and avoids a special-case revenue path.
