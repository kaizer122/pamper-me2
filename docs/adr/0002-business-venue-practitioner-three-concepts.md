# Provider is modeled as three concepts: Business, Venue, Practitioner

Rather than a single "Provider" entity, the platform splits the concept into Business (contractual and billing entity), Venue (physical location where service happens), and Practitioner (the person performing the service, owns a calendar). A solo freelancer is a Business with one Venue and one Practitioner; a salon chain is one Business with multiple Venues; mobile and home-studio services fit the same model with different Venue location types. We considered modeling everything as a single "Provider" with optional staff, but rejected it because clients overwhelmingly book a specific person, so Practitioner must be first-class. The word "Provider" is a marketing/UI term only — never used in code or domain discussions.
