# Reviews are Booking-verified, multi-dimensional, and Practitioner aggregates follow the person

A Review is a single record per `completed` Booking, capturing three numeric ratings (Business, Practitioner, Venue) plus a free-text comment. Submission is gated by Booking completion; the window is 14 days after the Booking end; publication is automatic with post-moderation; the Business may post one public response. Review text remains permanently attributed to the (Business, Practitioner, Venue) tuple at the time of the Booking — never rewritten when a Practitioner moves to a new Business. Aggregate Practitioner ratings, however, are computed across all of that Practitioner's `completed` Bookings regardless of which Business they were employed by, so a Practitioner's reputation is portable across moves. We rejected the alternative (reviews stay with the Business, Practitioners get no portable reputation) because it disadvantages independent and solo Businesses, who are the dominant segment in Tunis. This is not a violation of ADR-0006 — Reviews are public marketplace content, not Business-private commercial data.
